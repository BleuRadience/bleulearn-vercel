Grades K-2 | All Science Lessons

Use this guide with the BleuLearn Primary Lab Sheet.

📋 Lesson Structure (45 minutes)

TimeActivityTeacher DoesStudents Do
5 minOpeningAsk question, show objectListen, share predictions
10 minHands-On LabDemonstrate, guideObserve, explore
15 minWorksheetDistribute Primary Lab SheetDraw, write observations
10 minDiscussionAsk "What did you notice?"Share findings
5 minBleuLearn PauseAsk reflection questionWrite or draw reflection
🗣️ OPENING SCRIPT:
"Today we are going to be scientists! Our question is: [state question]. What do you already know about this?"
🌟 SCIENTIST SPOTLIGHT:
"Today's scientist is [name]. They discovered [contribution]. They show us that anyone can be a scientist!"

🧪 Sample Lessons

Lesson: Plants (Grade K)
Question: What do plants need to grow?
Materials: 2 cups, soil, bean seeds, water
Scientist Spotlight: George Washington Carver
Lesson: Sink or Float (Grade 1)
Question: Which objects sink and which float?
Materials: Water tub, rock, cork, coin, apple
Scientist Spotlight: Dr. Mae Jemison
Lesson: States of Matter (Grade 2)
Question: How does water change when we heat or cool it?
Materials: Ice cube, cup, warm water, plate
Scientist Spotlight: Dr. Percy Julian

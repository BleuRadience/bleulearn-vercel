🌍 Key Concept: Map Projections

Maps are not perfect. The Mercator projection makes Africa look the same size as Greenland -- Africa is 14 times larger. Gall-Peters shows true size but distorts shape. Teach students that every map has a documented perspective.

📚 Topics by Grade Band

K-2: Maps vs. globes, compass rose, land vs. water, continent names
3-5: US geography (states, capitals, regions), world continents, major oceans, climate zones
6-8: World regions, physical geography, human geography, documented Indigenous territories, map projections

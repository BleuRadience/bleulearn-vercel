5 minBleuLearn Pause + Action"What can you do?"Write letter or take documented action

⚖️ Voting Rights Timeline -- Must Teach

1776: Only white men with property can vote. 1870: 15th Amendment (Black men -- poll taxes and literacy tests block it). 1920: 19th Amendment (women -- Indigenous and Asian women excluded). 1965: Voting Rights Act. 2013: Shelby County v. Holder guts preclearance. Document every date.

📚 Key Concepts by Grade Band

5-6: What is government? Why do we need laws? Local government
7-8: Three branches, checks and balances, how a bill becomes a law, documented voting rights history
9-12: Constitution (including documented critiques), landmark Supreme Court cases, civil disobedience, documented civic action

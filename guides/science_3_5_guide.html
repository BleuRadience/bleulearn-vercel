🔬 Sample: Ecosystems
"Build a food web" -- Give students pictures of organisms and string. Connect predator to prey.
🔬 Sample: Oobleck
Cornstarch + water. Solid or liquid? Both! Discuss non-Newtonian fluids.
🔬 Sample: Crayon Rock Cycle
Shave crayons (sediments), press (sedimentary), warm (metamorphic), melt (igneous).

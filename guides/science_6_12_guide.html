🔬 Biology: Osmosis in Eggs
Eggs in vinegar (remove shell), corn syrup (water leaves), water (water enters). Measure mass changes.
🔬 Chemistry: Density Column
Layer honey, corn syrup, dish soap, water, oil, rubbing alcohol. Drop objects to see density layers.
🔬 Physics: Acceleration on a Ramp
Marble on ramp at different heights. Measure time, calculate acceleration. Graph height vs. acceleration.

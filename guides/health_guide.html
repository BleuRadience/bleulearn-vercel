📋 Lesson Structure (30-45 minutes, weekly)

TimeActivityTeacher DoesStudents Do
5 minCheck-inAsk "How are you feeling today?"Circle emotion, share
10 minTopic IntroductionTeach one conceptListen, ask questions
15 minDiscussion / ActivityLead breathing exercise or movementParticipate, share
5 minGoal Setting"What is one wellness goal?"Write documented goal
5 minBleuLearn Pause"What will you do to take care of yourself?"Write reflection

📚 Topics by Grade Band

K-2: Body awareness, feelings, movement, sleep, healthy eating
3-5: Nutrition basics, physical activity, sleep importance, stress management, friendship
6-8: Puberty, mental health, consent, substance abuse prevention, body image
9-12: Advanced mental health, sexual health, healthy relationships, stress management, documentation of wellness

🧘 Breathing Exercises

Box Breathing: Inhale 4 seconds, hold 4, exhale 4, hold 4. Repeat 4 times.
Five Finger Breathing: Trace fingers. Inhale up, exhale down each finger.
Balloon Breathing: Hands on belly. Inhale (belly grows like a balloon), exhale (balloon deflates).

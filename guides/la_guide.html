📚 BleuLearn Language Arts Educator Guide

Grades K-12 | All Reading and Writing Lessons

Use this guide with the BleuLearn Reading Response and Writing Paper.

📋 Lesson Structure (45-60 minutes)

TimeActivityTeacher DoesStudents Do
10 minRead-AloudRead a diverse book aloudListen, turn and talk
15 minComprehension DiscussionAsk who, what, where, when, why, howAnswer, ask questions
15 minWriting ConnectionModel writing, give promptWrite or draw response
5 minShareCall on 2-3 studentsRead or show work
5 minBleuLearn Pause"What did you learn today?"Write reflection

📚 Diverse Book List

Grades K-2: The Day You Begin, Last Stop on Market Street, Hair Love, We Are Water Protectors
Grades 3-5: One Crazy Summer, Esperanza Rising, The Crossover, Inside Out and Back Again
Grades 6-8: Brown Girl Dreaming, Ghost, The Hate U Give, March (graphic trilogy)
Grades 9-12: Their Eyes Were Watching God, Beloved, The Poet X, Between the World and Me

✍️ Writing Prompts

Narrative: "Tell me about a time you did something for the first time."
Opinion: "What is one rule you would change at school? Why?"
Informational: "What is something you know a lot about? Teach me."
Letter Writing: "Write a letter to someone who has helped you."
Poetry: "Write a poem about your name. What does it mean?"

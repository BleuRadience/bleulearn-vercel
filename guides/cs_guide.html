🌟 Documented Computer Scientists of Color

Katherine Johnson (1918-2020): NASA mathematician. Calculated trajectories for Apollo missions. Medal of Freedom 2015.
Dr. Shirley Ann Jackson (1946-): First Black woman PhD from MIT (physics). Invented technologies for caller ID and fiber optics.
Mark Dean (1957-): Co-invented the personal computer (IBM PC). Holds 3 of the original 9 PC patents.
Dr. Ayanna Howard (1972-): Roboticist. Designs AI for therapy and space exploration.
Kimberly Bryant (1967-): Founder of Black Girls Code. 30,000+ girls trained.

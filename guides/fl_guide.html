🗣️ Key Concepts by Grade Band

K-2: Identifying coins, needs vs. wants, saving in a jar
3-5: Earning money, making change, simple budgets, banking basics
6-8: Bank accounts, interest, credit vs. debit, documented racial wealth gap
9-12: Investing, retirement, taxes, entrepreneurship, generational wealth, cooperative economics

🌟 Racial Wealth Gap -- Must Teach (Grades 6-12)

The documented median white family has 8x the wealth of the documented median Black family (Federal Reserve, 2022). This comes from documented history: slavery, sharecropping, redlining, GI Bill exclusion, mass incarceration. Document every fact. No undocumented claims.

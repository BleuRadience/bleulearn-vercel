⏸️ THE BLEULEARN PAUSE

Think About Your Thinking (Metacognition)

📝 AFTER EVERY 3 PROBLEMS

STOP. WRITE ONE SENTENCE ABOUT HOW YOU SOLVED IT.

✅ WHEN YOU KNOW THE ANSWER
  • "I knew to add because ___"
  • "I drew a bar model first because ___"
  • "I checked my answer by ___"
🤔 WHEN YOU'RE STUCK
  • "My first attempt failed because ___"
  • "I was confused by ___"
  • "I need to practice ___ more"

📐 Singapore Math Bar Model Template

Mathematics  |  Grades 1-12

📄 Document used in this model answer: Problem: median white family wealth $171,000 vs. median Black family wealth $17,000 (Federal Reserve, 2022). If three Black families pooled wealth cooperatively, how much closer to one white family's wealth would they be?

✅ Model Answer

Step 1 bar (Equal Groups): Three equal sections, each labeled $17,000. Total bracket above: ?. Equation: 3 × $17,000 = $51,000.

Step 2 bar (Comparison): Top bar $171,000. Bottom bar $51,000. Difference bracket: ?. Equation: $171,000 − $51,000 = $120,000.

Answer with context: Cooperative pooling brings three families to $51,000 -- still $120,000 short. The 8:1 wealth ratio narrows to roughly 3.4:1 through pooling alone.

✔ Must Be Present for Full Credit

  • Bar model drawn BEFORE the equation, every time.
  • Bars are proportional to the values they represent.
  • Every part of the bar is labeled.
  • Answer includes units and a sentence of real-world context.

⚠️ Documented Common Errors for This Specific Worksheet

  • Writing the equation before drawing the bar.
  • Drawing all bars the same size regardless of value.
  • Omitting labels inside the bar parts.
  • Missing units in the final answer.

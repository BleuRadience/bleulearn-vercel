💰 Family Financial Plan

Financial Architecture  |  Grades 6-12

📄 Document used in this model answer: Household of 4, documented monthly income $4,500.

✅ Model Answer

Net worth: Assets $7,000 minus liabilities $18,000 = −$11,000.

Strategy: $100/month in a low-cost index fund at a documented historical 7% average return compounds to approximately $113,000 in 30 years.

10-year goal: Build a 6-month emergency fund ($26,100) within 2 years, then begin index investing, projecting roughly $50,000 net worth after 10 years.

✔ Must Be Present for Full Credit

  • All figures sourced (Federal Reserve, USDA, BLS).
  • Strategy includes an actual compound-interest calculation, not just 'save more.'
  • Goal is specific and numeric, not aspirational language only.

⚠️ Documented Common Errors for This Specific Worksheet

  • Invented figures with no source.
  • A 'strategy' with no math behind it.

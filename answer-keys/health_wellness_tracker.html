WS17 ANSWER KEY

🧠 Wellness Tracker

Health & Wellness  |  Grades K-12

📄 Document used in this model answer: Sample documented wellness week for a middle school scholar.

✅ Model Answer

Sleep: Average 8 hrs/night (target 8-10 for middle school, AASM).

Pattern noticed: Energy levels directly track sleep hours -- the lowest-sleep day (6.5 hrs) was also the lowest-energy day.

BleuLearn Pause plan: Set a 9:30pm wind-down alarm specifically on practice nights, the documented worst night for sleep.

✔ Must Be Present for Full Credit

  • Specific numbers recorded, not vague impressions.
  • A pattern is identified FROM the data, not assumed.
  • The plan is concrete (a specific time, a specific trigger), not 'I'll try to sleep more.'

⚠️ Documented Common Errors for This Specific Worksheet

  • Recording some days and skipping others with no explanation.
  • A plan so vague it can't be checked next week.

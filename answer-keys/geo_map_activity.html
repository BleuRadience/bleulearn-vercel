WS15 ANSWER KEY

🌍 Map Activity

Geography  |  Grades 3-8

📄 Document used in this model answer: Region: Powhatan Confederacy territory, Virginia, circa 1607.

✅ Model Answer

Documented territory: Eastern Virginia and the Chesapeake Bay region; capital at Werowocomoco on the York River.

Source: native-land.ca and the Virginia Department of Historic Resources (dhr.virginia.gov).

Geography-people connection: Control of river access points meant control of trade routes -- explaining why Jamestown's 1607 settlement was a documented strategic threat, not just a foreign curiosity.

✔ Must Be Present for Full Credit

  • Indigenous territory is mapped BEFORE the colonial label 'Virginia' is treated as default.
  • A documented source (native-land.ca or equivalent) is cited.
  • Geography is connected to documented human activity, not just landforms.

⚠️ Documented Common Errors for This Specific Worksheet

  • Map that centers the colonial settlement as the primary landmark.
  • No citation for territory boundaries.

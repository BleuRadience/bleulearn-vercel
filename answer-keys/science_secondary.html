WS10 ANSWER KEY

🔬 Science Lab (6-12)

Science  |  Grades 6-12

📄 Document used in this model answer: Investigation: osmosis in potato cylinders across five salt concentrations.

✅ Model Answer

Background: Osmosis moves water across a membrane toward higher solute concentration (Khan Academy, khanacademy.org).

Hypothesis: Above 1.0% salt, potato mass decreases, because water moves out of cells toward the higher-concentration solution.

Data: 0%: +0.8g. 0.5%: +0.3g. 1.0%: 0.0g (isotonic point). 1.5%: -0.4g. 2.0%: -0.9g.

Reasoning: The 1.0% equilibrium is the isotonic point matching natural potato-cell salinity; below it water enters cells, above it water leaves.

Sources of error: Cylinder size variance despite uniform cutting; ambient temperature; scale accuracy ±0.1g.

✔ Must Be Present for Full Credit

  • Background cites a specific source.
  • Hypothesis names the mechanism (osmosis), not just an outcome.
  • Sources of error are specific, never just 'human error.'

⚠️ Documented Common Errors for This Specific Worksheet

  • Background without citation.
  • Hypothesis without mechanism.
  • Generic 'human error' as the only listed source of error.

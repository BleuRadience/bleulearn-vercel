WS9 ANSWER KEY

🔬 Science Lab (3-5)

Science  |  Grades 3-5

📄 Document used in this model answer: Investigation: does ramp height affect how far a marble rolls?

✅ Model Answer

Hypothesis: If ramp height increases, the marble rolls farther, because higher ramps store more potential energy.

Variables: Independent: ramp height (5/10/15cm). Dependent: roll distance. Controlled: same marble, ramp, floor, release point.

Data: 5cm avg 25cm. 10cm avg 48cm. 15cm avg 71cm.

CER -- Claim: Increasing ramp height increases roll distance. Evidence: Distance rose from 25 to 48 to 71cm as height tripled. Reasoning: Higher ramps store more potential energy (mgh), which converts to kinetic energy on release.

✔ Must Be Present for Full Credit

  • Hypothesis uses If-Then-Because.
  • Three trials averaged per condition, not one.
  • CER reasoning explains the mechanism, not just common sense.

⚠️ Documented Common Errors for This Specific Worksheet

  • Hypothesis without the 'because' clause.
  • Single trial per condition.
  • Reasoning that just repeats the claim.

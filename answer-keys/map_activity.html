WS21 ANSWER KEY

🦅 Indigenous Territory Map

Indigenous Studies  |  Grades 5-12

📄 Document used in this model answer: The Pamunkey Nation territory, Virginia.

✅ Model Answer

Source: native-land.ca and pamunkey.org (official tribal documentation).

Pre-contact history: Part of the Powhatan Confederacy (Tsenacomoco), ~30 Algonquin-speaking nations at the time of 1607 English contact.

Contemporary status: Federally recognized 2015; documented treaty rights from the 1677 Treaty of Middle Plantation; oldest continuously occupied reservation in the U.S.

✔ Must Be Present for Full Credit

  • Territory documented with native-land.ca or equivalent.
  • Contemporary federal recognition status is explicitly stated, not omitted.
  • History includes the nation's own governance, not only what colonizers did to it.

⚠️ Documented Common Errors for This Specific Worksheet

  • Treating the nation as historical only, with no present-day status.
  • History told entirely from the colonial perspective.

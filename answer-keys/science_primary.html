WS8 ANSWER KEY

🔬 Science Lab (K-2)

Science  |  Grades K-2

📄 Document used in this model answer: Investigation: do plants grow healthier with sunlight or in the dark? (5-day bean sprout comparison)

✅ Model Answer

Question: Do plants grow taller and healthier in sunlight or in the dark?

Prediction: Sunlight plants will be healthier because plants need light to make food.

Observation Day 5: Sunlight plant: 4cm, green leaves. Dark plant: 3cm, yellow and thin.

Conclusion: The sunlight plant grew healthier. Light is necessary for healthy growth, not just height.

Scientist Spotlight: George Washington Carver -- used documented experiments with plants to develop hundreds of products that helped real communities.

✔ Must Be Present for Full Credit

  • Prediction includes a reason, not just a guess.
  • Observation distinguishes what was actually seen from what was expected.
  • Conclusion matches the data, even if it surprises the predicted outcome.

⚠️ Documented Common Errors for This Specific Worksheet

  • Observation that states the expectation rather than what was seen.
  • Conclusion that contradicts the data but is written anyway.

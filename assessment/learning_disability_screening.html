Learning Difference Screening

Early identification tool. This is NOT a diagnosis. If screening indicates a potential learning difference, refer to a licensed educational psychologist or neuropsychologist for formal evaluation.

Important Notice

This screening tool is an early identification support only. AvaBleu House HQ does not diagnose learning disabilities. Results should be shared with a qualified specialist. Black children are documented as disproportionately misdiagnosed and over-referred for special education. This tool is designed to identify documented strengths and documented areas for support -- not to label.

Screening Categories

Reading & Phonological Processing

Associated with dyslexia. Administered by teacher.

Working Memory & Processing Speed

Associated with ADHD and processing disorders.

Mathematical Reasoning

Associated with dyscalculia.

Written Expression

Associated with dysgraphia.

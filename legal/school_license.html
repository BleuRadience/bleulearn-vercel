Two-Entity IP Protection Structure

The BleuLearn Sovereign Curriculum operates under a documented two-entity structure:

  • Community-owned 501(c)(3): AuraBleu Academy -- the school. Serves students. Owned by the cooperative community.
  • AvaBleu House HQ: Curriculum IP owner. Licenses BleuLearn to the school. Retains all intellectual property.

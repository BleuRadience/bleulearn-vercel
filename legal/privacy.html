Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

AvaBleu House HQ is committed to protecting student privacy.

What We Collect

  • Student name and grade (when provided)
  • Learning progress data (stored locally in browser)
  • Points earned (stored locally in browser)

What We Do NOT Do

  • We do not sell student data
  • We do not share student data with third parties for advertising
  • We do not transmit student data to any server without explicit consent

FERPA and COPPA

BleuLearn complies with the Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act (FERPA) and the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). Parents may request access, correction, or deletion by contacting the school administrator.

Local Storage

Student progress is stored in your browser's local storage. Clearing browser data will reset progress. No server-side accounts are created without explicit enrollment.
